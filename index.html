SFSU Day of Civic Hacking

The Challenge

For the 2016 SF Day of Civic Hacking at SF State, we created an interactive district map of San Francisco focused on San Francisco Unified School District's (SFUSD) demographics and community-based programs that increase retention and graduation rates for youth in San Francisco.

The Department of Children, Youth and their Families (DCYF) helps to support over 50,000 youth and their families in 450 programs in every San Francisco neighborhood.
